    About the Pomodoro Timer

    This Pomodoro Timer helps you manage your time more effectively by dividing it into focus sessions and breaks. Start a 25-minute focus session by clicking the 'Focus' button. After each session, take a short break by clicking the 'Short Break' button, or a long break after four focus sessions by clicking the 'Long Break' button. The timer tracks the number of focus sessions and completed cycles to help you maintain a balanced work-rest schedule.
